# Toriv's Tarot App 🀧

A brief description of what this project does and who it's for

This Project is a small Tarot Card Reader hosted temporarily by Peanut's character, Snoopy. You ask a Question and you receive a Short Reading.

## How does it Work?

In this Project, you are briefly introduced to the concept of Tarot and Tarot Reading. Our temporary host, Snoopy, will quickly go over the basics and then ask you for a question. Following spiritual rules, you keep the question in your mind and the question gets energetically added on to an array.

You click on The Selected Card and are asked to take a short pause to both have the user breathe and the cards to "shuffle". You are then returned a Card alongside a short reading.
